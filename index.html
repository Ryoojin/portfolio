A little background

I came to data the long way — and that's the point.

I studied political science and journalism at Dongguk University, then earned a Master of Science in Business Analytics at Baruch's Zicklin School of Business. That path — from understanding people and stories to modeling their behavior — is exactly how I approach products: start with the human, prove it with data.

Today I lead a retail POS platform at KISS Products as a business analyst and product designer, owning requirements, UX, prototyping, and QA. Earlier I built NLP features at S&P Global, taught Data Visualization and Python as a graduate teaching assistant at Baruch, and managed ₩1B in annual ad campaigns at Buddhist Broadcasting System.
